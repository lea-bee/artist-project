Why A.C.E?

A.C.E (에이스) is a 5-member South Korean boy group under Beat Interactive, consisting of Park Junhee, Lee Donghun, Wow, Kim Byeongkwan, and Kang Yuchan. On February 5, 2021, it was announced that the group will be co-managed by SWING Entertainment. They debuted on May 23, 2017, with their first single, CACTUS.

Name Meaning: A.C.E stands for Adventure Calling Emotions, meaning that the group wants to cause emotions that urge people to go on adventures and make the dreams come true. The name is pronounced as “ace”.

Official Greeting: “Set! To be a hero, A.C.E! Hello, we are A.C.E!”
A.C.E Official Fandom Name: 초이스 (Choice)
